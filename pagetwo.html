About

Kitties to Go! was founded in 2012 by two cat lovers. We wanted everyone to know the benefits of snuggling with a friendly cat and hearing their purrs. Kitties to Go! is committed to providing the best customer service and support. From the begining we knew cat lovers wanted to have a great experience with their furry friends. We are committed to supporting non profits who care for cats that have yet to find a home. 10% of our profits from each Kitty time is donated to a local shelter and veterinary clinic so they can get the best care possible.
